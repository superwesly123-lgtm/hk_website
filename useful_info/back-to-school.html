‘English Assessment for P1 Admission’ Platform

‘English assessment for P1 admission’ Platform

由楊教授開發的「小一入學英語評估」平台，識別小一學生的英語水平，以提供針對性的增潤和補救教學。

The "English Assessment for P1 Admission" platform, developed by Prof YEUNG identifies Primary 1 students' English proficiency to English proficiency to enable target enrichment programme for advanced learners and focused remediation strategies for those needing additional support.

背景

Background

合作夥伴：

Partnership:

七所香港佛教協會小學與香港教育大學楊教授合作，開發在線評估平台，以應對小一學生在英語能力方面的顯著學習者多樣性。

Seven Hong Kong Buddhist Association primary schools collaborated with Prof. Susanna Yeung from the Education University of Hong Kong to develop an online assessment platform to address significant learner diversity in English proficiency among Primary 1 students.

平台擴展：

Platform Expansion:

該平台建立在楊教授的應用程式「Back to School Life」之上，這是一個專為後疫情時代從幼兒園過渡到小學的兒童設計的綜合學習支援和準備資源。

The platform built upon Prof. Yeung's application "Back to School Life", a comprehensive learning support and preparation resource specifically designed for children transitioning from kindergarten to primary school in the post-pandemic era.

數據驅動洞察：

Data-Driven Insights:

利用大規模小一英語學習能力數據，該平台為教育進步和學生發展提供寶貴的見解。

Utilizing large scale Primary 1 English learning competency data, the platform offers valuable insights for educational advancement and student development.

平台功能

Platform Features

評估小一學生的9項關鍵基礎英語能力，以指導教師設計針對性的教學策略。

Assess 9 key fundamental English competencies of Primary 1 students to guide teachers in designing tailored teaching strategies.

  1. 字母識別
  2. Letter recognition
  3. 詞彙測試
  4. Vocabulary test
  5. 句子理解
  6. Sentence comprehension
  7. 字素-音素知識
  8. Grapheme-phoneme knowledge
  9. 語音意識（音節、韻律、音素）
  10. Phonological awareness (syllable, rhyme, phoneme)
  11. 交際英語（指令理解）
  12. Communicative English (instruction comprehension)
  13. 單詞閱讀
  14. Word reading
  15. 學習動機
  16. Learning motivation
  17. 閱讀動機
  18. Reading motivation

研究影響

Research Impact

教育影響：

Educational impact:

  • 超過2,000名學生接受了初步英語語言和讀寫能力評估。
  • Over 2,000 students assessed on initial English language and literacy skills.
  • 超過40位教師利用評估數據來定制教學。
  • More than 40 teachers utilized assessment data to customize instruction.
