向日葵繪本閱讀計劃

Sunflower Reading Programme

透過精選繪本培養兒童閱讀興趣，提升語言能力和想像力的創新閱讀計劃。

The Sunflower Reading Programme, developed by Dr. LIN, aims to nurture children's enjoyment and skills in reading, supporting their language development.

計劃概述

Background

基於證據的在線平台：

Evidence-based online platform:

  • 計劃基礎：向日葵繪本閱讀計劃是基於林博士20年研究成果的轉化而開發，專注於發展兒童的讀寫能力和正面心理發展。
  • Program Foundation: The Sunflower Reading Programme was developed based on the transformation of Dr. LIN's 20-year research findings, focusing on developing children's literacy skills and positive psychological development.
  • 教學資源：向日葵繪本閱讀計劃採用團隊精心開發的繪本，融入兒童正面發展的核心元素。同時精心挑選覆蓋率高的中文字詞，配合幼稚園和小學低年級學生的中文水平。
  • Teaching Resources: The Sunflower Reading Programme adopts carefully developed picture books by the team, which incorporate core elements of children's positive development. It also carefully selects Chinese characters and words with a high coverage rate, matching the Chinese proficiency of kindergarten and lower primary school students.
  • 實施支援：團隊由經驗豐富的教師組成，持有香港幼兒教育證書並擁有心理學學位。這些教師負責錄製視頻、開發課程，並上傳至在線互動學習平台供學生自主學習。
  • Implementation Support: The team consists of experienced teachers who hold Hong Kong Early Childhood Education Certificates and have psychology degrees. These teachers are responsible for recording videos, developing courses, and uploading them to an online interactive learning platform for students' self-directed learning.

平台特色

Platform Features

  • 20多本原創繪本、40多節課程和70多個常用字，輕鬆掌握。
  • 20+ original picture books, 40+ lessons, and over 70 commonly used characters, easily mastered.
  • 互動遊戲鞏固學習成果。
  • Interactive games to reinforce learning outcomes.
  • 輕鬆識字，學習拆字組詞等。
  • Easily recognize characters and learn to break down characters and form words, etc.

平台特色

Platform Features

  • 閱讀故事、識別中文字和學習心理學。
  • Read stories, recognize Chinese characters, and learn about psychology.
  • 在線自主學習平台，時間靈活。
  • Online self-learning platform with flexible timing.
  • 融入兒童發展元素，如正面心理學、社交情緒和自我發展。
  • Incorporate elements of child development, such as positive psychology, social emotions, and self-development.
