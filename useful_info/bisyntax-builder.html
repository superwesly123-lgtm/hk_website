Bisyntax Builder

Bisyntax Builder

專為雙語學習者設計的句法意識培養工具，提升中英文句子結構理解和運用能力。

Bisyntax builder, developed by Dr. TONG, is an innovative language learning tool that focuses on helping learners understand and master bilingual syntactic structures. This platform aims to promote learners' language development and comprehension abilities through interactive methods.

工具介紹

Background

Bisyntax Builder 是結合科學研究證據開發的英語語法自學程式，適用於高小及初中學生，共有 20 個學習單元。學生可以在自學程式上上進行對比式學習，在每個單元中，學生需要完成不同的練習，包括句子結構分析、語法規則學習、多個語法主題，設有「Knowledge Bank」，可隨時回顧每個單元的重點知識，幫助學生鞏固多樣化的練習題，包括選擇題、句子重寫及選擇題，運用及鞏固語法知識。

Research Findings:

Dr. TONG's previous research revealed that syntactic knowledge is the cornerstone of reading comprehension in both Chinese and English, and it is also associated with reading comprehension difficulties among Hong Kong Chinese - English bilingual children.

Reading Performance Connection:

Children with good syntactic knowledge show good reading comprehension performance. They can improve reading by acquiring proficient English syntactic skills through Bisyntax Builder.

研究背景與開發理念

Platform features

香港教育大學心理學系副教授徐秀博士及團隊致力於行為及認知神經科學面向的第二語言習得研究。基於多年的實證研究成果，團隊開發了 Bisyntax Builder 是一款自修博士及其團隊基於科學研究證據開發，專為學習英語語法第二語言的學生而設計的英語語法自學程式，運用人工智能，程式會根據學生的學習進度和能力，提供個人化的學習建議和練習。

  • • Online Self-learning: Freely choose when and where to learn.
  • • In-class and After-class Practice: Diversified exercises (including sentence rewriting, multiple-choice questions, etc.) help apply and reinforce grammar knowledge.

核心功能與特色

Platform features

透過中英文語法結構對比，幫助學生理解語言差異

  • • Comparative Learning: Deepen understanding of English grammar by drawing comparisons with Chinese grammar.
  • • Original Animated Teaching Videos: Replay to enhance learning effectiveness.
  • • 20 Learning Units: Comprehensive learning content as well as various grammar topics. There's a "Knowledge Bank" for user to review the key knowledge of each unit at any time.

Educational Impact:

  • • Over 400 students have actively used the App.
  • • 5 schools recommended the App as a free out-of-classroom learning tool for students, parents, and teachers.
  • • Offers 20 structured Learning Units with a built-in "Knowledge Bank" for review.

Positive qualitative feedback from students and parents:

  • User-friendly & Engaging: Students with reading challenges are able to navigate and benefit from the App effectively.
  • Flexible & Intuitive: Students can freely choose when and where to learn, using Chinese grammar comparisons to deepen their understanding of English grammar.
  • Stimulating Rewards: The historical statistics system helped preserve students' active participation.
