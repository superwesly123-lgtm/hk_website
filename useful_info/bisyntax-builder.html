Research Findings:

Dr. TONG's previous research revealed that syntactic knowledge is the cornerstone of reading comprehension in both Chinese and English, and it is also associated with reading comprehension difficulties among Hong Kong Chinese - English bilingual children.

Reading Performance Connection:

Children with good syntactic knowledge show good reading comprehension performance. They can improve reading by acquiring proficient English syntactic skills through Bisyntax Builder.
