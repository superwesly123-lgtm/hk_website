Social Robot Joey

Social Robot Joey

The AI-driven social robot Joey, developed by Prof YEUNG, enhances children's English language development through interactive storytelling learning activities.

📚 背景

📚 Background

  • 首個專為學校環境中的幼兒設計的社交機器人，用於提供故事講述活動
  • First social robot designed to deliver storytelling activities for young children in school settings
  • 建立在社交機器人、教育心理學和語言學習的前沿知識之上
  • Built on cutting-edge knowledge from social robotics, educational psychology, and language learning
  • 社交機器人：提供學習陪伴，支持兒童成長
  • Social Robotics: Provides learning companionship to support children's growth
  • 教育心理學：創造社交夥伴關係以促進學習
  • Educational Psychology: Creates social partnership to facilitate learning
  • 語言學習：使用故事講述作為關鍵策略，增強溝通技能和知識傳遞
  • Language Learning: Uses storytelling as a key strategy to enhance communication skills and knowledge transfer
  • 促進幼兒的英語語言學習體驗
  • Promote English language learning experience in young children

教育工具「Joey」特色

Educational Tool 'Joey' Features

  • 使用故事講述和復述方法的自動化鷹架語言學習策略
  • Automated Scaffolded Language Learning Strategy using storytelling and retelling methods
  • 真實溝通：同步面部表情和手臂動作，實現真實的社交互動
  • Authentic Communication: synchronizes facial expressions and arm gestures for genuine social interaction
  • AI 驅動的後端系統：生成定制化學習內容，包括故事音頻、插圖和理解問題
  • AI-powered Backend System: generates customized learning content with story audio, illustrations and comprehension questions
  • 智能反饋系統：捕捉語言和手勢反應，提供鷹架式協助和反饋
  • Smart Feedback System: Captures verbal and gestural responses for scaffolded assistance and feedback

研究影響

Research Impact

  • 👥 超過 2,000 名年輕英語學習者與 Joey 互動
  • 👥 2,000+ Young English Learners Engaged with Joey
  • 🏫 40 所學校受惠，包括 16 所小學和 24 所幼稚園
  • 🏫 40 Schools Benefited with 16 Primary Schools and 24 Kindergartens
  • 👨‍🏫 超過 200 名幼稚園及小學教師接受培訓
  • 👨‍🏫 200+ Teachers Trained in Kindergartens & Primary Schools
  • 📅 此項目正在進行中，預計於 2026 年 8 月完成。
  • 📅 The project is ongoing and will be completed in August 2026.

教育工具「Joey」的有效性

Effectiveness of the Educational Tool 'Joey'

研究證據（Yeung et al., 2025）顯示：

Research evidence (Yeung et al., 2025) showed:

  • 社交機器人改善兒童的故事理解和參與度，相比使用平板電腦。
  • Social robot improved children's story comprehension and engagement compared to using tablets.
  • 社交機器人優於人類和平板電腦，能夠持續維持兒童的注意力。
  • Social robot outperforms humans and tablets in sustaining children's attention over time.
  • Joey，一個類人機器人，比機械外觀的機器人更能提升理解力和注意力。
  • Joey, a human-like robot, enhances comprehension and attention better than machine-looking robots.
