Reading Step-by-Step: Affix platform

Reading Step-by-Step: Affix platform

系統性的閱讀能力培養計劃，從基礎到進階，逐步提升學生的閱讀理解能力。

The Reading Step-by-Step: Affix platform, developed Prof YEUNG, is a progressive reading ability development project that focuses on gradually improving learners' reading comprehension and analytical abilities in stages. This project particularly focuses on learners with reading difficulties, providing personalized learning paths.

📚 Background

📚 Background

  • Reading Step-by-Step: Affix 平台是基於當前閱讀科學開發的，該科學強調形態意識（MA）是英語讀寫能力的關鍵——尤其對於英語作為第二語言（ESL）的學習者。
  • The Reading Step-by-Step: Affix platform was developed based on current reading science, which highlights morphological awareness (MA) as key to English literacy—especially for English as a Second Language (ESL) learners.
  • 有研究支持，MA 通過促進單詞閱讀來提高閱讀理解能力。詞素幫助學習者識別單詞邊界，並為解碼和理解詞彙提供有意義的線索。
  • Backed by research, MA improves reading comprehension by facilitating word reading. Morphemes help learners identify word boundaries and provide meaningful cues for decoding and understanding vocabulary.

平台特色

Platform Features

  • 為中國小學生提升英語技能：形態意識、閱讀和理解能力。
  • Boosts English skills for Chinese primary students: morphological awareness, reading, and comprehension.
  • 13個模塊涵蓋25個英語詞綴。
  • 13 modules covering 25 English affixes.
  • 引人入勝的內容，包含互動教學視頻、練習和遊戲。
  • Engaging content with interactive teaching videos, exercises, and games.
  • 從單詞到句子再到文本層面的漸進式難度。
  • Progressive difficulty from word to sentence to text level.
