在幼兒中，第二語言的詞彙、句法意識和閱讀理解能促進彼此的發展嗎？

Do vocabulary, syntactic awareness, and reading comprehension in second language facilitate the development of each other in young children?

Xie, Q., & Yeung, S. S. (2022)

Learning and Instruction, 82, 101682

本研究指出，兒童的第二語言 (L2) 詞彙和句法意識是兒童閱讀理解能力增長的重要預測因素。這意味著教師應該在英語課中強調詞彙和句法結構意識。詞彙和句法意識差的學生可能會在閱讀理解上遇到困難。此外,鑒於先前的研究結果，我們建議教師在訓練閱讀能力時加入詞彙和句法意識教學，以語境來推斷生詞的含義。我們也建議老師多給孩子一些時間練習閱讀，以加強他們的閱讀理解能力，提高綜合語言能力。

This study explored whether children's second language (L2) vocabulary, syntactic awareness, and reading comprehension contributed to the growth of each other. A total of 184 Chinese primary school children (91 girls) aged 8–10 years old in Hong Kong participated in the pre-test of this study. Among them, 88 were in Grade 3 and 96 were in Grade 4. One year later, 178 of these children also participated in the post-test. These children learned English as an L2 at school. They completed a series of English language tasks. The results from a cross-lagged panel model show that vocabulary predicted the growth of syntactic awareness and reading comprehension. Syntactic awareness predicted the growth of vocabulary and reading comprehension. Reading comprehension facilitated the growth of vocabulary, and it also predicted the growth of syntactic awareness in Grade 4 students but not in Grade 3 students. Implications for teaching children an L2 are discussed.

存在閱讀障礙風險的第二語言學習者：一項增長混合模型研究

Second language learners who are at-risk for reading disabilities: A growth mixture model study

Yeung, S. S. (2018)

Research in Developmental Disabilities, 78, 35-43. doi: 10.1016/j.ridd.2018.05.001

這項為期一年的縱向研究考察了學習英語作為第二語言（ESL）的中國兒童的英語閱讀發展軌跡，並確定了有英語閱讀障礙風險的兒童的認知狀況。來自香港八所幼兒園的一百八十四名中國 ESL 兒童在幼兒園最後一年接受了四次測量，測量內容包括語音意識、字母知識、詞彙和英語單字閱讀。應用成長混合模型，根據兒童在英語單字閱讀方面的成長軌跡對他們進行分類。詞彙閱讀成長被分為四個子組，分別是高成就組、快速成長組、慢速成長組和低成就組。不同群體的認知語言技能進行了比較，以年齡、非語言智力和 L1 接受詞彙為對照。研究結果顯示，成績較差的群體（預計面臨二語閱讀障礙的風險）在字母名稱知識、音素意識以及接受性和表達性詞彙方面存在缺陷。在測量認知語言技能時，快速成長群體和高成就群體沒有區別。低成長組兒童的語音意識、接受性詞彙和表達性詞彙明顯弱於高成就組兒童。我們的研究結果發現了與有閱讀障礙風險的兒童相關的特定認知語言缺陷。討論了對早期識別 L2 閱讀障礙的意義。

This one-year longitudinal study examined the developmental trajectories of English reading in Chinese children learning English as a second language (ESL) and identified cognitive profiles of children who are at risk for English reading disability. One hundred and eighty-four Chinese ESL children from eight Hong Kong kindergartens were measured four times during their last year of kindergarten for phonological awareness, letter knowledge, vocabulary and English word reading. Growth mixture modeling was applied to classify the children based on their growth trajectories in English word reading. Four subgroups of word reading growth were classified, namely high-achieving, fast-growth, slow-growth and low-achieving groups. The cognitive-linguistic skills were compared across different groups with age, non-verbal intelligence and receptive vocabulary in L1 controlled. The results showed that low-achieving groups, who were expected to be at-risk for L2 reading disability, showed deficits in letter-name knowledge, phonemic awareness, and receptive and expressive vocabulary. Fast-growth and high-achieving groups were not distinguishable on the measured cognitive-linguistic skills. Children in the low-growth groups were significantly weaker in phonemic awareness, receptive vocabulary and expressive vocabulary than children in the high-achieving group. Our findings identified specific cognitive-linguistic deficits that were associated with children who are at-risk for reading disability. Implications for the early identification of L2 reading disability were discussed.

漢字認讀能力與正字法意識關係的發展性變化：以漢語為例

Developmental Changes in the Relationship Between Character Reading Ability and Orthographic Awareness in Chinese

Lin, D., Mo., J., Liu, Y., & Li, H. (2019)

Frontiers in Psychology, 10, 2397

本研究透過兩個獨立的樣本，檢視了從幼兒園一年級到小學三年級，六年間漢字閱讀能力與正字法意識之間關係的發展變化：幼兒園樣本為96名兒童，在幼兒園一年級、二年級和三年級（K1、K2、K3）進行三次評估，間隔12個月。對 204 名小學兒童樣本進行了四次評估，評估分別在一年級第一學期和第二學期（P1-S1；P1-S2）、二年級第一學期（P2-S1）和三年級（P3-S1）進行，前三輪評估間隔 6 個月，最後一輪評估間隔 12 個月。交叉滯後路徑分析顯示漢字閱讀與正字法意識關係的發展分為三個階段。在第 1 階段，K1 和 K2 的閱讀能力可預測 K2 和 K3 的正字法意識。在第 2 階段，從 P1-S1 到 P1-S2，字符閱讀和正字法意識之間存在雙向關係。在第 3 階段，P1-S2 和 P2-S1 時的正字法意識可以預測 P2-S1 和 P3-S1 時的後續字元閱讀能力，但從閱讀到正字法意識的預測在此階段消失了。研究結果描繪了漢字閱讀和正字法意識之間關係隨時間變化的完整發展圖像。隨著閱讀能力的提高，初學者在發現或提取正字法法規方面表現出了令人印象深刻的能力。

The present study examined developmental changes, over a 6-year period, in the relationship between character reading ability and orthographic awareness in Chinese from the first year of kindergarten to the third year of primary school in two separate samples: the kindergarten sample of 96 children was assessed three times in the first, second, and third years of kindergarten (K1, K2, K3) with 12-month intervals. The primary school sample of 204 children was assessed four times in the first and second semesters of grade 1 (P1-S1; P1-S2), first semester of grade 2 (P2-S1) and grade 3 (P3-S1), with the first three waves at 6-month intervals and the final wave at 12-month interval. Cross-lagged path analysis showed three developmental stages of the relationship between Chinese character reading and orthographic awareness. At stage 1, reading ability in K1 and K2 predicted subsequent orthographic awareness in K2 and K3. At stage 2, there was a bidirectional relationship between character reading and orthographic awareness from P1-S1 to P1-S2. At stage 3, orthographic awareness at P1-S2 and P2-S1 predicted subsequent character reading ability at P2-S1 and P3-S1, but the prediction from reading to orthographic awareness vanished at this stage. The results depict a full developmental picture of the changed relationship between Chinese character reading and orthographic awareness over time. Beginning readers demonstrated impressive abilities in discovering or extracting orthographic regularities with increased reading ability.

詞素意識對漢字認讀能力成長速度的預測作用

Morphological awareness predicts the growth rate of Chinese character reading

Lin, D., Sun. H., & McBride. C. (2019)

Developmental Science, 22, e12793.

在這項縱向研究中，我們對 88 名香港處於典型發展階段的幼兒園兒童的漢字閱讀準確率進行了四次評估，每次間隔 6 個月，為期 1.5 年。第一次測驗是在幼兒園第二年（K2）的秋季，在此期間測試了形態意識、語音意識、正字法意識、視覺空間關係和非語言智商（時間 1）。潛在成長曲線模型顯示，典型發展中的幼兒園兒童的中文閱讀發展遵循累積的線性軌跡，這表明初始閱讀能力較高的兒童閱讀能力發展速度更快。此外，K2 階段的形態意識積極且獨特地預測了 K2 和 K3 之間在 1.5 年內（香港進行正式中文教學和學習的時期）漢字閱讀的線性增長模式。這些研究結果為文獻做出了貢獻，強調了形態意識在閱讀增長率中的獨特意義：通常，早期形態意識較好的兒童往往具有更高的閱讀能力起點，更重要的是，增長率更快，從而導致低績效者和高績效者之間的發展結果差異更大。研究結果表明，應更加重視早期讀者形態意識的發展，因為形態意識在漢語閱讀發展中起著重要作用。

In this longitudinal study, we assessed 88 Hong Kong Chinese typically developing kindergarteners' Chinese character reading accuracy four times with 6-month intervals over 1.5 years with the first testing point in the fall of the second year of kindergarten (K2), during which morphological awareness, phonological awareness, orthographic awareness, visual–spatial relationships, and nonverbal IQ were tested (Time 1). The latent growth curve modeling showed that reading development in Chinese of typically developing kindergarteners followed a cumulative linear trajectory, suggesting that children with higher initial reading ability develop reading ability at a faster rate. Additionally, morphological awareness at K2 positively and uniquely predicted a linear growth pattern of character reading between K2 and K3 over 1.5 years, a period in which formal teaching and learning Chinese takes place in Hong Kong. Contributing to the literature, these findings highlighted the unique significance of morphological awareness in the growth rate of reading: Typically developing children with better early morphological awareness tend to have a higher initial point of reading ability and, more importantly, a faster growth rate, resulting in a wider discrepancy of developmental outcomes between low- and high- performers. The results suggest that greater attention should be focused on the development of morphological awareness in early readers, given its salient role in Chinese reading development.

句法技能與閱讀理解之關聯性統合分析：跨語言與發展性探究

A Meta-Analysis of the Relation Between Syntactic Skills and Reading Comprehension: A Cross-Linguistic and Developmental Investigation

Tong, X., Yu, L., & Deacon, S. H. (2024)

Review of Educational Research, 00346543241228185.

閱讀理解理論普遍預測了句法技能（即理解和運用句子結構的能力）的作用。然而，這些理論主要基於英語，因此對於這種理論是否適用於不同類型語言（例如英語和漢語）的問題，目前尚無定論。漢語和英語的句子結構存在顯著差異，因此兩者的比較尤為有趣。 我們進行了一項薈萃分析，旨在對比英語和漢語母語讀者的句法技能與閱讀理解之間的關係。我們測試了語言以及年級和指標對這種關係大小的影響。研究納入了1986年至2021年發表共59項研究，產生了234個效應量，涉及15,212名從幼稚園到高中及以上的參與者。 研究結果顯示，對於英語（r = 0.54）和漢語（r = 0.54）讀者的研究，其效應量非常相似，在關鍵發展階段和句法任務上也存在相似性。此外，還有一些證據表明，年級水平和句法任務的性質會對此產生影響。 這些發現證實了基於理論的預測，即句法技能對閱讀理解的重要性。擴展這些預測，證明這些影響對英語和漢語都適用，表明句法技能對閱讀理解具有普遍的影響。

Theories of reading comprehension have widely predicted a role for syntactic skills, or the ability to understand and manipulate the structure of a sentence. Yet, these theories are based primarily on English, leaving open the question of whether this remains true across typologically different languages such as English versus Chinese. There are substantial differences in the sentence structures of Chinese versus English, making the comparison of the two particularly interesting. We conducted a meta-analysis contrasting the relation between syntactic skills and reading comprehension in first language readers of English versus Chinese. We test the influence of languages as well as the influence of the grade and the metrics on the magnitude of this relation. We identified 59 studies published between 1986 and 2021, generating 234 effect sizes involving 15,212 participants from kindergarten to high school and above. The magnitude of effects was remarkably similar for studies of English (r = .54) and Chinese (r = .54) readers, with similarities at key developmental points and syntactic tasks. There was also some evidence of modulation by grade levels and the nature of syntactic tasks. These findings confirm theory-based predictions of the importance of syntactic skills to reading comprehension. Extending these predictions, demonstrating these effects for both English and Chinese suggests a universal influence of syntactic skills on reading comprehension.

中英雙語四年級學童如何調用句法意識促進閱讀理解：語言內與跨語言效應探究

How Chinese–English Bilingual Fourth Graders Draw on Syntactic Awareness in Reading Comprehension: Within- and Cross-Language Effects

Tong, X., Kwan, J. L. Y., Xiuli Tong, S., & Deacon, S. H. (2022)

Reading Research Quarterly, 57(2), 409-429.

句法或句子結構在閱讀理解中起著重要作用，但學生如何在閱讀中運用對句法的意識仍不清楚；對於雙語學生來說，這種機制就更加不明確了。在本研究中，我們評估了句法意識對 227 名香港中英雙語四年級學生的第一語言中文和第二語言英語閱讀理解的直接和間接貢獻。我們設計了語言共享和語言獨特的句法意識任務，評估了中文和英文的閱讀理解能力，並採取了其他與閱讀相關的認知和後設語言測量。我們發現，無論是第一語言漢語或第二語言英語，句法意識對閱讀理解都有顯著的直接影響，同時透過詞彙閱讀也產生了間接影響。此外，在英語和漢語的閱讀理解中，學生更多地利用了英語和漢語共同的句法特徵，而不是兩種語言獨有的句法特徵。學生對語言共享專案的理解通常比對語言獨特專案的理解更準確，這進一步表明了遷移的可能性。我們的研究結果闡明了句法意識在漢語和英語閱讀理解中的雙重作用，以及兩種類型不同的語言中句法結構意識的轉移。我們結合閱讀理解和遷移理論來討論這些結果。

While syntax or sentence structure plays a crucial role in reading comprehension, how students utilize their awareness of syntax during reading remains unclear. This mechanism is even more ambiguous for bilingual students. In this study, we evaluated the direct and indirect contributions of syntactic awareness to reading comprehension in both their first language (L1) Chinese and second language (L2) English for 227 Hong Kong Chinese-English bilingual fourth-grade students. We designed both language-shared and language-specific syntactic awareness tasks, assessed reading comprehension in both Chinese and English, and included other reading-related cognitive and metalinguistic measures. We found that syntactic awareness had a significant direct impact on reading comprehension in both L1 Chinese and L2 English. It also had an indirect effect through word reading. Furthermore, students utilized syntactic features common to both English and Chinese more than language-unique syntactic features in their reading comprehension for both languages. Students generally comprehended language-shared items more accurately than language-unique items, further suggesting the possibility of transfer. Our findings illuminate the dual role of syntactic awareness in both Chinese and English reading comprehension, as well as the transfer of syntactic structure awareness across two typologically different languages. We discuss these results by integrating theories of reading comprehension and transfer.
