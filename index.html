第二語言學習及介入

Enhancing Children's Language Learning through Training and Technology

楊、林和唐三位學者在四個關鍵領域對兒童中文和英文的語言與讀寫能力發展產生了顯著影響：

  • 提升學習者的中英文語言與讀寫能力：透過實施一系列基於實證的線上（plugged）和線下（unplugged）干預措施，這些干預不僅改變了課堂內外的教學實踐，還有效地提升了學生的中英文語言與讀寫技能。
  • 指導家長在家中提供支援：透過與參與我們項目的家長進行密切溝通與合作，告知他們如何在家中以最佳實踐方式支援孩子的語言和讀寫能力發展。
  • 改進中英文教學：為學校課程設計提供寶貴建議，從而改善中英文教學品質。
  • 支援有閱讀困難的學生：透過多種方法，例如為特殊教育需求（SEN）學校的教師提供培訓課程，來支援有閱讀困難的學生。
  • 將干預計劃商業化：特別是涉及社交機器人的項目，旨在創造一個對兒童友好的語言學習環境。

透過出版、培訓、媒體分享和工具商業化等多種途徑，這些影響力體現在促進兒童的發展以及改變教師和家長的實踐方式上。

YEUNG, LIN and TONG have generated significant impacts on children's Chinese and English language and literacy development across four key areas:

  • Enhancing Chinese and English language and literacy skills among learners by implementing a range of evidence-based plugged and unplugged interventions, which have transformed teaching practices both inside and outside the classroom.
  • Informing parents about best practices for supporting language and literacy development at home through close communication and collaboration with parents involved in our projects.
  • Improving Chinese and English instruction by providing valuable suggestions for the design of school curricula.
  • Supporting students with reading difficulties through various approaches, such as delivering training sessions for teachers in special education needs (SEN) schools.
  • Commercializing our intervention programs, particularly those involving social robots, to foster a child-friendly language learning environment.

Through a wide range of reach including publication, training, media sharing and commercialization of tools, impacts were manifested through enhanced development of children and changing practice of teachers and parents.

我們的理念

Our Rationale

應對早期語言教育的關鍵挑戰，為兒童、教育工作者和家庭創造有意義的學習體驗。

Addressing key challenges in early language education and foster meaningful learning experiences for children, educators, and families.

有限的語言接觸

Limited Language Exposure

在香港，教師缺乏高質量的語言接觸，課堂之外的語言學習機會有限。

Teachers lack high-quality language exposure outside the classroom in Hong Kong.

參與度挑戰

Engagement Challenges

缺乏個性化和互動體驗，導致學習動機和參與度下降。

Addressing the lack of personalized and interactive experiences to boost motivation and engagement.

教師工作量

Teacher Workload

減少教師在準備個性化教學材料方面的時間投入。

Reducing the time investment for teachers in preparing tailored teaching materials.
