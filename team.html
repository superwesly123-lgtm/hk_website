楊少詩博士

Dr. Yeung Siu Sze Susanna

團隊總監

Team Director

楊少詩博士是心理學系的副教授。她一直在教育心理學、發展心理學、認知心理學和有關有特殊需要的幼兒等領域任教。她是我們的團隊負責人，她主要負責有關與第二語言學習、閱讀介入、實證為本、學生學習和雙語能力發展相關的研究項目。她的論文是關於以英語為第二語言的幼兒的語音意識與閱讀之間的關係。她的研究圍繞幼兒的語言和第二語言的閱讀發展。楊博士其他研究重點包括與語言和閱讀發展相關的情感和認知的因素以及閱讀介入的效果。目前，她的研究調查兒童的正常和非典型英語閱讀發展、認知語言基礎和第二語言學習的情感過程以及家庭環境在語言學習中的作用。她還研究瞭解如何在各種技術平台上優化學習。

Dr. Yeung Siu-sze is an Associate Professor in the Department of Psychology. She has been teaching in the areas of educational psychology, developmental psychology, cognitive psychology, and young children with special needs. She is our team leader and is primarily responsible for research projects related to second language learning, reading intervention, evidence-based practices, student learning, and bilingual development. Her research examines the effects of morphological awareness, orthographic awareness, and phonological awareness in Chinese (L1) and English (L2) word reading and reading comprehension in Chinese children with longitudinal and intervention approaches. These metalinguistic skills have been found to be crucial in developing reading skills in Chinese learners. Currently, her research investigates children's typical and atypical English reading development, cognitive-linguistic foundations and affective processes of second language learning, and the role of home environment in language learning. She also studies how to optimize learning on various technology platforms.

Team Member

佟秀紅博士

Dr. TONG Xiuhong

佟秀紅博士是一位發展心理學家。她於2013年獲得香港中文大學心理學系博士學位，並於2016年完成博士後訓練。她的主要研究興趣集中在語言學習、閱讀發展及相關困難，並採用多種方法進行研究。2016年至2017年間，她在杭州師範大學擔任研究教授。她已撰寫或合著近40篇期刊文章及3個書籍章節。

Dr Xiuhong Tong is a developmental psychologist. Her main research interests focus on language learning and reading development and difficulties with multiple approaches. She received her Ph.D. degree in the Department of Psychology from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2013. She then completed her postdoctoral training at CUHK in 2016. She worked as a research professor at Hangzhou Normal University from 2016-2017. She has authored or coauthored nearly 40 journal articles and 3 book chapters.

林丹博士

Dr. LIN Dan

林丹博士是香港教育大學心理學系的副教授。她是一位發展心理學家，主要研究領域是識字能力發展。她以首席研究員身份獲得了三項傑出青年學者計劃/優配研究金（ECS/GRF）資助。她擔任超過15個專業期刊以及香港研究資助局（RGC）和美國國家科學基金會（NSF）資助項目的顧問編輯或審稿人。此外，她也擔任香港學術及職業資歷評審局的學術專家。

Dr Lin Dan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Psychology at The Education University of Hong Kong. She a developmental psychologist with research interests mainly in literacy development. She has been awarded three ECS/GRF grant as the Principal Investigator. She serves as consulting editor/reviewers for 15+ professional journals as well as for Hong Kong RGC and U.S. NSF grants. She additionally serves as a specialist for the Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of Academic & Vocational Qualifications.
